PHASE 5 GOAL:
Make Velora MVP demo-ready and grant-ready.

* Wallet authentication
* Merchant dashboards
* Payment links
* Real Solana Devnet payments
* Transaction receipts
* Merchant payment history
* Merchant profiles
* Merchant settings
* Branded checkout foundation

Phase 5 should polish the MVP into a product that can be shown to grant reviewers, investors, and early users.

* Google or Apple sign-in

* Mainnet support
* Full product catalog

* Real logo upload/storage
